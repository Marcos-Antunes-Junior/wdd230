Purpose

Salvador is the capital of Bahia, which has 2.9 million inhabitants. During festivals such as Carnival, more than 15 million people circulate in this city. We at Barra Lighthouse develop activities that aim to stimulate commerce here in Salvador. We assist companies looking to enter the local market by informing them of current business conditions and trade regulations. If you are interested in growing together with the great capital of Bahia, Salvador, join us.
