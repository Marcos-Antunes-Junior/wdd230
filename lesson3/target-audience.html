Audience

People aged 25+ who are looking to start a new business in a thriving location, or who already own a business and want to open new franchises. They will be able to access the site via smartphone, tablet, laptop and desktop.

Persona 1 and Scenario

Lucas is 35 years old and has a degree in Business Management. Four years ago, he opened two technology stores, where he sells smartphones, laptops, desktops, and computer hardware. Lucas wants to open a third store in Salvador, as he knows there is a large flow of people and that it would be a great investment. He also wants to strengthen and receive support from local businesses so that he can succeed. For this reason, while searching on Google and social media, Lucas found the Barra Lighthouse website and was able to join us in achieving his goal.

Persona 2 and Scenario

Amanda is 45 years old and owns a grocery store in the neighborhood of Brotas, Salvador. She manages to sell her products every month but is not making progress in her business. When looking for resources to move her business forward, Amanda found the Barra Lighthouse website on Google, where she was very interested in participating to strengthen her local business.
